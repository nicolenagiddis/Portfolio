Visual Identity System

ABOUT

Shown are my corporate branding elements for the UNESCO Word Heritage Site of the Great Barrier Reef. The reef's organization is focused on protecting the reef from climate change, educating people about the wildlife and how to protect it,and updating the public whenever the reef is doing good or bad. Click any image to view up close.

GOALS

I wanted to make a brand that reflects the importance of protecting the Great Barrier Reef, while also having a fun and colorful presence. I chose bold fonts reflecting the importance paired with colors and imagery inspired by the reef.
