Poster Series

ABOUT

These posters are the result of an object study done throughout the semester of spring 2025. My object was a travel-sized bottle of Victoria's Secret "Bare" perfume. The assignment was very open-ended, so I created my posters in a way that could be an ad, but also could be something else entirely. Click on an image to view up close.

GOALS

Through my object study, I discovered that perfume is a strong form of confidence that is often overlooked. I wanted to convey the feeling of confidence while keeping the product hidden, as if you had to unlock it, hence the "key" to your confidence.
